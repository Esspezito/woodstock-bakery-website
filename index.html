Our Story

Founded in 2013 by Paul Hartmann and Paul Cremer, born from a unique mentor-student relationship

Our Philosophy

"We follow traditional bread-making methods using the best local ingredients, handling them as little as possible from scaling to firing. We prioritize nutritional content over commercial efficiency."

Every day, our team of 20+ passionate craftspeople hand-shapes up to 800 loaves in our wood-fired ovens. Our 48-hour sourdough process and commitment to stone-ground flour has earned us the trust of 85+ top Cape Town restaurants.

From our original location at The Biscuit Mill to our current home in Salt River, we've remained committed to the ancient craft of bread making, bringing authentic artisan breads to Cape Town's tables.
