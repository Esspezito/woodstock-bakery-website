A Decade of Artisan Excellence

Founded in April 2013 by Paul Hartmann and Paul Cremer, Woodstock Bakery was born from a unique mentor-student relationship that began at the South African Chefs Academy.

Our journey started when Paul Cremer, a 2007 graduate of Paul Hartmann's culinary academy, completed a 3-year apprenticeship at the legendary Ile de Pain in Knysna. Six years later, mentor and student united their passion to create something extraordinary in Cape Town's vibrant food scene.

Our Philosophy

"We follow traditional bread-making methods using the best local ingredients, handling them as little as possible from scaling to firing. We prioritize nutritional content over commercial efficiency, rejecting high-speed processing that robs flour of all its flavor."

Every day, our team of 20+ passionate craftspeople hand-shapes up to 800 loaves in our wood-fired ovens. Our 48-hour sourdough process and commitment to stone-ground flour has earned us the trust of 85+ top Cape Town restaurants, including Luke Dale-Roberts' Naturalis and Chef's Warehouse.
