A Decade of Artisan Excellence

Founded in April 2013 by Paul Hartmann and Paul Cremer, Woodstock Bakery was born from a unique mentor-student relationship that began at the South African Chefs Academy.

Our Philosophy

"We follow traditional bread-making methods using the best local ingredients, handling them as little as possible from scaling to firing. We prioritize nutritional content over commercial efficiency."

Meet the Founders

Paul Hartmann

Co-Founder & Master Baker

Established chef and educator who founded the South African Chefs Academy. Paul's vision of traditional craftsmanship drives our commitment to authentic bread-making methods.

Paul Cremer

Co-Founder & Artisan Baker

Academy graduate (2007) who honed his craft through a 3-year apprenticeship at Ile de Pain in Knysna. Paul brings precision and passion to every loaf we create.
